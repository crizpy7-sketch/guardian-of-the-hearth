# The Ultimate Execution Blueprint (Phase 6)

Everything you need to *do the thing*. Brand 2.0 turned into a calendar, scripts, and lists.

## A. The Top 10 Actions That Will Produce the Most Sales

Ranked by probability of moving revenue for a new RGV boutique. Do them in this order.

1. **Make Shia Songs™ the hero and film "first-listen" reactions weekly.** Your only true
   category-of-one product + the most shareable footage you can make. This is the wedge.
2. **Optimize the Google Business Profile and farm reviews to 4.8★ / 50+.** Highest-ROI
   free action; captures ready-to-buy local searchers and walk-ins.
3. **Capture phone + email at every single touchpoint** (register, site, "DM us," giveaways).
   The owned list is your future revenue. Start day one.
4. **Launch the Baby Milestone Club** (due date + birthday → automated SMS/email). Turns a
   one-time buyer into a multi-year customer in the category with natural repeat.
5. **Post short-form video daily (TikTok + Reels), bilingual, hook-first.** The discovery
   engine. Treat the first 30 videos as research; double down on winners.
6. **Productize 4–5 named, fixed-price gift bundles** and make them the default ask. Lifts
   AOV from ~$45 toward $89–$150 and kills decision fatigue.
7. **Run a grand-opening / giveaway campaign** to seed the list, the community, and a month
   of content in one weekend.
8. **Build a Spanish-first + grandparent track** (gift cards, phone/FB ordering, concierge).
   Unlocks the highest-AOV, most loyal segment in the RGV.
9. **Stand up the referral program** ("Give $10, get $10" / free keepsake). Cheapest growth;
   babies travel in social packs.
10. **Boost only proven-winner videos with small paid spend** (TikTok Spark + Meta), tightly
    geo-targeted. Let organic prove conversion before scaling paid.

> If you could only do three: **#1 (Shia Songs hero video), #2 (GBP + reviews), #3 (capture
> the list).** These alone will outperform most boutiques' entire plans.

## F. The Perfect Grand Opening Strategy

**Goal:** one weekend that seeds the list, the community, a month of content, and local fame.

**4 weeks out — build anticipation & the list**
- "Coming soon" everywhere; countdown Stories; a VIP "first-look" SMS/email signup.
- Launch the **giveaway**: *"Win a $250 Shia & Co. dream bundle + a free Shia Song — follow,
  tag 2 expecting/new moms, share to your story."* (Reach + list explosion.)
- Seed 5–10 RGV creators with product + private invites *before* opening.
- Invite press (The Monitor, local morning TV, RGV lifestyle pages) + McAllen Chamber for a
  **ribbon-cutting** (free press).

**Launch weekend — manufacture the moment**
- Ribbon-cutting, balloons, the photo wall, free wrapping.
- **First 50 guests get a free keepsake**; live **Shia Songs** demos at a listening station.
- Raffle entry = phone + email capture (build the list).
- Live-stream on FB/IG; assign someone to film *everything* (a month of content).
- "Founding Family" perk: first Milestone Club members get a permanent small perk (loyalty +
  urgency).
- Bilingual hosting throughout. **ES**

**Launch offers**
- Grand-opening gift with purchase; bundle promos; limited "opening weekend only" drop.

**Week after — convert the spike**
- Welcome-flow SMS/email to every new contact.
- Post the recap reel; repost all attendee tags; announce the giveaway winner live.

**Budget:** $1,000–$3,000 one-time (decor, giveaway, refreshments, small ad push, press).
**Expected:** follower spike, a seeded owned list, a content library, and citywide awareness.

## G. Monthly Advertising Budget Plan

**Phase 1 — Ramp (Months 1–3): ~$850/mo. Prove conversion before scaling.**

| Channel | Spend | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Meta (FB+IG) | $300 | Find creative winners; grandparents/gift buyers |
| TikTok Spark | $200 | Boost proven organic winners only |
| Google Search | $150 | Capture high-intent local searches |
| Influencer/UGC | $150 (mostly product) | Content volume + borrowed trust |
| Local/community | $50 | Booth/partnership seeds |
| Email/SMS tool | ~$30 | Owned channel |

**Phase 2 — Scale (Months 4–9): ~$1,500/mo, weighted to proven winners.**
Increase whatever returned best in Phase 1 (often Meta + TikTok Spark + Google). Cut
laggards. Add retargeting + lookalikes off the now-seasoned list/pixel.

**Phase 3 — Seasonal push (Q4 / Mother's Day / shower season): +50–100% for 4–8 weeks.**
Holiday gifting is peak. Front-load budget into "First Christmas" + gift bundles + Shia Song
gift cards.

**Hard rules:**
- Don't scale a channel until it shows positive ROAS with real data (~30–60 days).
- Keep paid CAC under ~⅓ of first-order gross profit; let LTV (repeat) justify more later.
- Free/owned channels (GBP, reviews, list, organic video, local) come first — they make
  paid cheaper by warming the audience.

## H. The Full Customer Journey (discovery → lifelong loyalty)

**Stage 1 — Discovery (stranger).**
She's scrolling and a Shia Songs first-listen video stops her thumb. She didn't know she
needed it; now she's crying at a stranger's baby. *Touchpoint:* TikTok/Reel. *Goal:* the
follow + the save.

**Stage 2 — Curiosity (follower).**
She checks the profile, sees the Ritual, the reviews, the bilingual warmth, the prices
("from $25" — relief). She follows, watches Stories, maybe joins via a lead magnet ("hear a
sample song"). *Touchpoint:* IG profile, Highlights, GBP reviews. *Goal:* get on the list.

**Stage 3 — Consideration (lead).**
She's on the SMS/email list. Welcome flow gives 10% off. A friend mentions Shia (referral),
or a creator she trusts posts it. She searches "baby shower gift mcallen," finds the GBP,
50 five-star reviews. Trust crosses the line. *Goal:* first purchase.

**Stage 4 — First purchase (customer).**
She buys a bundle (AOV ~$89) or a Shia Song, in-store or online. The Ritual + handwritten
note + fast fulfillment (≤48h) exceed expectations. She photographs it and posts it — she
just became marketing. *Touchpoint:* store/site + packaging. *Goal:* delight + capture
due-date/birthday → Milestone Club.

**Stage 5 — Delight & advocacy (fan).**
Post-purchase: thank-you + review request + a keepsake cross-sell. She leaves a 5★ review,
joins Hearth Mamas, gets a referral code. She tells her pregnant friends. *Goal:* reviews +
referrals + community membership.

**Stage 6 — Repeat (loyal customer).**
The Milestone Club texts her exactly when baby outgrows a size or a milestone arrives (3mo,
6mo, 1st birthday). Birthday-month reward. New seasonal drops she anticipates. Each purchase
deepens the bond. *Goal:* multi-year repeat across the natural baby-growth cycle.

**Stage 7 — Lifelong loyalty (advocate / ambassador).**
She's now a Hearth Mama who defends and recruits. Maybe an ambassador. For her second baby,
Shia is the *default*. She gifts Shia at every shower she attends. The Growing-Up Song renews
each birthday — a bond that lasts as long as the child grows. *Goal:* she is the brand's
voice, and her LTV spans years and multiple families.

> **The whole journey in one line:** A stranger cries at a baby's song → follows → joins the
> list → trusts the reviews → buys a beautiful bundle → posts it → joins the tribe → gets
> reminded at every milestone → and spends the next decade gifting Shia & Co. to everyone she loves.

## Final word (the rules, honored)

- **Brutally honest:** your moat isn't "luxury" — it's Shia Songs + community + an owned
  list. Aesthetics are copyable; those three are not.
- **Not generic:** every tactic here is built for McAllen/RGV, bilingual, and a solo-owner's
  real time budget.
- **Highest-probability first:** the Top 10 (§A) are ordered by revenue impact. If
  overwhelmed, do #1–#3 and ignore everything else until they work.
- **Make it memorable:** the song, the Ritual, the first-listen face. Sell the moment, and
  the products sell themselves.
