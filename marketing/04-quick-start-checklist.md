# Shia & Co. — 1-Page Quick-Start Action Checklist

> Print this. Tick the boxes. If you only do what's on this page, you're ahead of 90% of
> boutiques. Full detail lives in the other files in this folder.

---

## 🚀 First 30 Days — Set Up the Machine (do once)

- [ ] Make **Shia Songs™** the hero product (every bio, sign, and post points to it)
- [ ] Fully optimize **Google Business Profile** (photos, hours, categories, products)
- [ ] Set up **email + SMS tool** (Klaviyo or Mailchimp) and the welcome flow
- [ ] Put a **phone/email capture** at the register ("10% off + first to know")
- [ ] Build the **Baby Milestone Club** signup (collect due date + birthday)
- [ ] Productize **4–5 named gift bundles** at fixed prices ($89 / $129 / etc.)
- [ ] Create the **in-store photo moment** + "tag us @shiababy 🤍" sign
- [ ] Lock the **Shia Ritual** packaging (cream box, wax seal, hand-written name card)
- [ ] Make everything **bilingual** (English + Spanish) — signs, posts, service
- [ ] Set the **fulfillment promise**: Shia Songs ≤48h, keepsakes ≤72h
- [ ] Launch the **referral program** ("Give $10, get $10")
- [ ] List **20–30 RGV mom-creators**; DM 5 to start gifting

---

## 📅 Every Day (~25 min)

- [ ] Post **1 short video** to TikTok + reuse as IG Reel (hook in first 1 second)
- [ ] Post **3–5 Stories** (behind-scenes, polls, new arrivals, customer reposts)
- [ ] **Reply to every comment & DM** (this feeds reach AND closes sales)
- [ ] **Pin 3–5** images to Pinterest
- [ ] Capture **every customer's phone + email**

## 🗓️ Every Week

- [ ] **Sunday:** batch-film 7–14 videos + schedule the week
- [ ] Post **1 Shia Songs first-listen** reaction video (your best content)
- [ ] Engage helpfully in **2–3 local RGV Facebook groups**
- [ ] **DM 3–5 creators**; send product to 1–2
- [ ] **1 Google Business update** + reply to all new reviews
- [ ] **Check ad spend** — cut losers, boost winners
- [ ] **Friday:** review analytics → double down on what got saved/shared/DM'd

## 📆 Every Month

- [ ] Review the 4 numbers: **AOV, margin, CAC, repeat rate**
- [ ] Refresh in-store displays / seasonal photo backdrop
- [ ] Plan next month's content calendar + budget
- [ ] Run **1 limited drop** ("only 12 made") for scarcity
- [ ] Send a **win-back** SMS to lapsed customers

---

## 🎯 The 3 Things That Matter Most (if overwhelmed, do only these)

1. **Film Shia Songs first-listen reactions** — your category of one + most shareable content
2. **Google Business Profile + farm reviews to 4.8★ / 50+** — captures ready-to-buy locals
3. **Capture the phone + email list** — the only audience you truly own
