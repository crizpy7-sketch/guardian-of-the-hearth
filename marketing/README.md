A complete, McAllen/RGV-grounded marketing system for **Shia Baby** (brand: **Shia & Co.**),
a luxury-feeling baby boutique. Built to be executed, not admired.

## The one-paragraph version (if you read nothing else)

Shia Baby cannot win on price or selection — chains and Amazon crush you there. You
win on **emotion, locality, and experience**: the keepsake that makes a grandmother
cry, the personalized baby song no big box can sell, the in-store gender-reveal moment
worth filming. Sell the *memory*, not the onesie. Be relentlessly bilingual (Spanish +
English), relentlessly local (McAllen, Edinburg, Mission, Pharr, Brownsville), and
relentlessly consistent on TikTok/Instagram/Reels with real faces and real moments.
Capture every customer's phone number and email at the register. Build a referral and
"first dollar" repeat system. Everything else is detail.

> Dollar figures are starting recommendations for a single-location boutique. Treat
> them as dials, not commandments — scale with what actually returns.
